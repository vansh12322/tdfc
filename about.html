Our Story

Founded in 2025, The Dry Fruit Company is a passion project turned successful business

Our Journey

Founded in 2025 by three passionate entrepreneurs - Vansh Ganjoo, Ayush Amul, and Yash Swami, The Dry Fruit Company began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide the highest quality dry fruits while promoting healthy living and sustainable practices.

What started as a small venture has grown into a trusted name in the dry fruits industry, serving customers across the nation with premium quality products and exceptional service.

  • Delivering exceptional customer service and satisfaction
